This mod adds a large amount of accessories, Yoyos, new mechanics, and changes to Vanilla Yoyos.

Adds over 30 more Yoyos, each with special crafting recipes and effects

Purchase the Ability Ring from the Merchant for 20 gold to allow yoyos to use special effects.

- Changes To Vanilla Yoyos
    Each Yoyo's stats have been buffed, including lifetime,
    speed, and range
    Yoyos have special particles

- Unique Accessories
   Rings
     Some rings make Yoyos emit particles and light
     Some rings add certain functions to Yoyos
   Bearings
     Yoyos can inflict debuffs, or create other projectiles
   Drill Casings
     Yoyos can drill through blocks
   New Yoyo Gloves
     Support Gloves : Increases yoyos made by the Yoyo Glove
     Yoyo Gloves : Function similarly to the regular Yoyo Glove,
     but have small differences

- 10 new accessory slots
    2 String Slots
    2 Bearing Slots
    2 Ring Slots
    2 Glove Slots
    Drill Slot
    Counterweight Slot

-= Many changes to Vanilla =-
  ( check out 'vanillaChanges.txt' in the mod's files)

This mod's focus is not entirely to be balanced, but to give a more enjoyable experience with using yoyos.
Some things may be tweaked later to help balance it out a little.
